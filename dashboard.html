About the job

We're looking for a driven Customer Success Manager to own relationships with a portfolio of US SaaS clients from Colombia. You'll work directly with decision-makers, drive product adoption, reduce churn, and expand accounts — all in English, fully remote, on US time zones.

This is a high-ownership role inside a fast-growing nearshore model. You won't be a middleman — you'll be the expert your clients rely on every day.

Responsibilities
  • Own a portfolio of 25–40 US SaaS accounts, serving as the primary point of contact
  • Drive onboarding, product adoption, and renewal conversations in English
  • Identify expansion opportunities and coordinate with the sales team
  • Monitor account health metrics and proactively intervene before churn
  • Conduct regular business reviews (weekly, monthly, quarterly) with clients
  • Document client needs and relay product feedback to internal teams
  • Maintain accurate records in CRM (Salesforce / HubSpot)
Skills we're looking for
CRM proficiency (Salesforce, HubSpot, or similar)
English C1 Advanced or higher (written & spoken)
SaaS business model understanding
Data analysis & reporting (Excel, Google Sheets)
Strong verbal and written communication
Problem-solving under pressure
US business culture awareness
Project coordination & time management

Qualifications
  • 2–5 years of experience in a Customer Success, Account Management, or client-facing SaaS role
  • Demonstrated ability to manage a book of business independently
  • Experience working with US-based teams or clients (preferred)
  • Professional English level — you'll be on calls with US stakeholders daily
  • Degree in Business, Communications, or related field (or equivalent experience)
  • Availability to work on Eastern or Central US time zone hours
